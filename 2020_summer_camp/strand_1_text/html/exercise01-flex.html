Which he did not see, but learned from reliable men in truth; and for this we will put the facts seen between the seen and those known by the voice among the known, so that our book is true and reliable | without any lies, and all those who read and hear will have to believe him, because they are all true facts. And I certify that after Our Lord made Adam, the first father, there was never a man of any lineage who knew or looked for in the different parts of the world or as this Messer Marco Polo knew; and for this reason he thought it would have been a great evil if he had not had what he had actually seen and heard recorded in writing, so that whoever has not seen or heard knows it thanks to this book. To know this he remained 27 years in these places; and when he was in the prison of Genoa he had this book written neatly by Messer Rasta of Pisa, who was in that prison, in the year 1298 of the Incarnation.
